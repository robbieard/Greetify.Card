# Audio Files for Gift Cards

Place your background music files here:

- `birthday.mp3` - Upbeat birthday music
- `romantic.mp3` - Romantic anniversary music  
- `celebration.mp3` - New Year celebration music
- `peaceful.mp3` - Peaceful Ramadhan music

## Recommended Audio Specifications:
- Format: MP3
- Duration: 30-60 seconds (will loop)
- Bitrate: 128kbps or higher
- Volume: Normalized to prevent loud playback

## Free Music Sources:
- Pixabay Music
- Freesound.org
- YouTube Audio Library
- Incompetech

Note: Ensure you have proper licensing for any music you use.
